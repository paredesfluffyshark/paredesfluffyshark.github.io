PROJECT DESCRIPTION
My pieces compiled in the portfolio titled Casted portray a total of four characters who were shaped from methodically writing down a daily journal entry daily and discerning ideas from these. This portfolio consists of a series of character sheets, GIFs and digital paintings in an attempt to capture the essence of the characters that I made. Though the characters were initially made from my own thought patterns, as I created the final pieces they blossomed into an exploration of the character themselves and how they are a refraction of myself rather than a reflection.
