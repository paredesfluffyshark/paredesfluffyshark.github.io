Unique Mundane Items

Unique Mundane Items is a video about 4 students comparing versions of the same items they have with eachother. As the item is passed around and through each zoom screen, the item changes and shifts, fitting the personalities and identities of each of the students. The seemingly mundane items suddenly become unique in the context of the student's preferences. This deceptively crude piece depicts individuality of each of the students in NYUAD.
